About

LateSkate is an award-winning safety illumination system for longboards.

Whether you're using it to look cool or stay safe, LateSkate is a great item to have. From its awesome and simple cross-platform App to its intuitive and automatic Brake and Bail lights, LateSkate is crafted with pure wonder in mind.
